Welcome To The Quiz

The quiz is a web-based form that contains a set of questions related to the Internet of Things (IoT) and personal information. The quiz is designed to test the user's knowledge and understanding of the basic concepts related to IoT, such as its definition, communication protocols, examples of IoT devices, security challenges, etc. The quiz consists of five multiple-choice and open-ended questions, as well as a personal information section, where the user is required to provide their first name, last name, and student ID. The quiz is interactive and allows the user to select and submit their answers for grading. The quiz serves as a useful tool for self-assessment and evaluation of one's knowledge of IoT concepts.
