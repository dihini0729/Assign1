IOT

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a network of physical devices, vehicles, buildings, and other items that are embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity, allowing them to collect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet. This technology enables the creation of a smart ecosystem where devices can interact with each other and with humans, and can be remotely monitored and controlled. The data generated by these devices can be analyzed to gain insights, optimize processes, and improve decision-making. IoT has applications in various fields such as healthcare, agriculture, transportation, manufacturing, and home automation, among others.
